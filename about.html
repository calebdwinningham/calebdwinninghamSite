about us.


Edge Case was founded with a simple goal in mind: solve hard problems. With access to both great minds and cutting-edge resources, edge case seeks to do just this. Applying research proven methodologies, problem scoping is a significant part of our solution process. It lays the foundation for all of our development and relies heavily on input from those like you, our partners. In the vast all-encompassing spectrum that is the software engineering industry, the teams assembled to tackle your specific issue are custom-formed with experts in the necessary fields solicited for distinct purposes. We pride ourselves on our quality, transparency, and dependability. For each project we take on, from beginning to end, we stake our reputation on our excellence. If these are the qualities you value, we look forward to hearing from you. Together, there is no limit to the challenges we can tackle.

about our founder.


Caleb Winningham is an Air Force Academy alumni with a knack for continuous process improvement. While a cadet, he studied Computer Science and fostered a passion for technologically-centric entrepreneurism. Upon graduation, this passion transitioned into practical application. Working with mentors and peers, Caleb went on to found an innovation-centered software development team. With projects to benefit both the Air Force at large in addition to products aimed at the private sector, Caleb and his team were able to develop full-stack applications at scale to conquer issues spanning from health-care to cybersecurity. In the past few years, this hobby has once again transitioned; now, he has taken this movement and converted it into a business: edge case consulting and software solutions, llc. With a collective of like minded minds, this company has been confronting an abundance of wicked problems head on. Will yours be next?
