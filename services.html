services.

We pride ourselves in our ability to tailor custom solutions to meet your unique challenges. From start to finish, our team will work alongside you to customize an answer to meet your most persistent problems head-on. While we stand confident and prepared to confront any challenge, our most commonly utilized services are touched on below.

There is no limit to what we can tackle as a collective.




collaborative needfinding.

Over a series of scoping sessions, the most complicated aspect of most any project can be put to rest: defining the actual root problem that must be resolved. Like a doctor diagnosing a patient, treating the cause and not the symptoms of a specific pain point provides the most value added to our customers. With our expert problem framers, the runway to a clear, impactful solution is laid out.






project pre-flights.

Once a problem is defined, the focus shifts to planning the actual solution. Our solutions architects will take the reigns at this point to curate a custom project proposal. This product will enable you to understand the clear end-state, feature break-down, anticipated cost, timeline expectations, and resource requirements. Regardless of whether you decide to utilize edge case to carry out your project, this document will enable any development team to hit the ground running.






software solutions.

Once you’re satisfied with the project proposal a team custom tailored to meet your specific requirements is assembled and gets to work. With regular progress meetings and feedback solicitation, the solution evolves in an agile and adaptive manner with the customer fully ingrained in the iterative development cycle. This enables heightened flexibility and a shared consciousness to grow amongst the group as a collective, and in most cases, leads to the ideal solution being produced. This is what edge case is all about.






after action support.

Just because your project is over doesn’t mean our relationship has to be. With the technological landscape ever changing, our final solutions are engineered with the future in mind. If a desire for expanded capabilities or compatibility arises, your product is postured for continued development. With many solutions, an element of support may also be required. If that’s the case, we’re happy to provide the service ourselves if we’re a good fit. If not, we’ll gladly connect you with a partner that’s more suitable.
